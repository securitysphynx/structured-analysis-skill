# Complete Taxonomy: 66 Structured Analytic Techniques

> **Source**: "Can get all 66 techniques with the cluster designations" (Gemini analysis)
> **Basis**: Third Edition, "Structured Analytic Techniques for Intelligence Analysis" (Pherson & Heuer, 2020)
> **Focus**: Complete enumeration of all 66 techniques organized by family
> **Reading Time**: ~8 minutes | **Audience**: All levels | **Prerequisites**: None

---

## Overview

The 3rd Edition expanded the original doctrine into 66 techniques organized into **six families** that follow the lifecycle of an intelligence project. Nine techniques are entirely new (marked with ★).

---
